# Retail Sales Dashboard Analysis
<img width="647" height="750" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a559168c-d53e-4fdb-a30f-aa5b85ee14a4" />


Dataset: Retail Sales Dataset
Schema: Star Schema with one Fact table (Sales) and multiple Dimension tables (Products, Regions, and Date).

**Univariate Analysis**
KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity Sold, Average Profit Margin, and Number of Transactions.

**Bivariate Analysis (Two-variable visual comparisons)**
Region-wise Sales and Profit (Map/Bar Chart)
Visualize total sales revenue and profit across different regions to identify top-performing areas.

Category-wise Sales (Pie Chart)
Display total sales by product category to determine which product segments contribute most to revenue.

Top 5 Products by Sales Amount (Column Chart)
Highlight the highest-selling products using a Top N filter to focus on key revenue drivers.

Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
Track total sales and profit trends monthly or quarterly to analyze growth patterns and seasonality.
**Multivariate Analysis (3 or more variables together)**


Sales Performance Analysis:
Compare total sales, profit, and quantity sold by combining filters for Region, Category, and Month through interactive slicers.
